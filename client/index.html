Strength

is one of the three primary attributes with the theme of defending against magic damage. It grants health, health regeneration, and magic resistance to every hero. Strength heroes also gain attack damage per point of strength.

Agility

Agility attribute is one of the three primary attributes that grants movement and attack speed and armor to every hero. Agility heroes also gain attack damage per point of agility.

Intelligent

is one of the three primary attributes with the theme of improving spell-casting. It grants mana, mana regeneration and spell damage amplification to every hero. Intelligence heroes also gain attack damage per point of intelligence.
